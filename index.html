What Makes Our Coffee Special?

Founded in 2017 by Mahi Singh, Roastery coffee was created with one goal: to create great and consistent coffee, and share it with as many people as possible. our aim is to source and roast some the best coffees available. By paying premium prices we enable coffee farmers to create sustainable business. It is one of our principles to treasure the value chain from crop to cup. All our coffees are scoring 86 points and more : they are fresh in season and roasted to perfection. As we have grown and changed over the years, we at roastery coffee found new ways to share flavour and connect communities across India.
